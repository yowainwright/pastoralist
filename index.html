Track security issues &
manage overrides
automatically

Pastoralist automatically tracks and cleans up security dependency issues AND your package.json overrides, resolutions, and patches.

bun add -g pastoralist

Works Seamlessly with Your Workflow

Pastoralist integrates perfectly into your development process. Run it as part of your CI/CD pipeline, include it in your pre-commit hooks, or use it manually whenever you need to update your dependency overrides.

Works With All Major Package Managers

Pastoralist works seamlessly with npm, yarn, pnpm, and bun package managers.

Install Pastoralist

Install pastoralist globally, as a devDependency,
or use with npx. or just use with npx or adjacent.

bun add -g pastoralist
